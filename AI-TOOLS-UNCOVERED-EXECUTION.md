## SCRIPT PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Weekly Batch (3-4 hours total)

**Monday: Research (1 hour)**
- Check Product Hunt for new AI tools
- Browse AI Twitter/Reddit for trends
- Check competitors: Futurepedia, There's An AI For That
- Pick 2-3 tools to review

**Tuesday: Scripts (1.5 hours)**
- Use your script templates
- Customize for specific tool
- Write hooks (spend 50% of time here)
- Create shot list

**Wednesday: Recording (1.5 hours)**
- Record voiceover (use AI voice or your own)
- Screen capture tool demos
- Gather B-roll (AI-generated or stock)

**Thursday: Editing (2-3 hours)**
- DaVinci Resolve or CapCut
- Add graphics/text overlays
- Create thumbnail
- Write title/description

**Friday: Upload & Optimize (30 min)**
- Upload to YouTube
- Add end screens + cards
- Schedule or publish
- Post to socials

## THUMBNAIL FORMULA

### Template Structure
1. **Background:** Gradient or AI-generated scene
2. **Main image:** Tool logo/screenshot or reaction face
3. **Big text:** 3-5 words max, bold font
4. **Accent element:** Arrow, circle, or emoji
5. **Brand consistency:** Same colors/fonts

### Color Psychology
- **Blue:** Trust, tech, professional
- **Orange/Yellow:** Attention, urgency
- **Green:** Money, growth, success
- **Red:** Warning, important, stop

### Text Formulas That Work
- "I tried [X] so you don't have to"
- "This [tool] changed everything"
- "[Number] AI tools for [result]"
- "Stop using [old way], use this"
- "Free [tool] beats paid alternatives"
