### SCRIPT #11: "I Tried [Topic] for 30 Days"
**Format:** Challenge/Vlog
**Length:** 12-18 minutes
**Hook:** "The results surprised me."

**INTRO (45 seconds)**
"What happens when you commit to [TOPIC] for 30 days straight? I decided to find out. No excuses, no skipping - just 30 days of [ACTION]. Here's what happened to my [METRIC]."

**THE SETUP (2 minutes)**
"Before we start, here's where I was: [BASELINE]. I tracked [METRICS] daily. My goal was [GOAL]. The rules were simple: [RULES]."

**WEEK 1: THE STRUGGLE (3 minutes)**
"Days 1-7 were rough. [DESCRIBE DIFFICULTIES]. I wanted to quit on day [X] because [REASON]. But I pushed through and by day 7, [FIRST IMPROVEMENT]."

**WEEK 2: ADJUSTMENT (3 minutes)**
"Week 2, things changed. [WHAT CHANGED]. I learned that [INSIGHT]. The biggest surprise? [SURPRISE]. My [METRIC] improved by [PERCENTAGE]."

**WEEKS 3-4: THE TRANSFORMATION (4 minutes)**
"By week 3, [POSITIVE CHANGE]. And week 4? [DRAMATIC RESULT]. Here's the data: [SHOW STATISTICS]. The difference is night and day."

**THE VERDICT (2 minutes)**
"Will I continue? [ANSWER]. Would I recommend it? [ANSWER WITH CONDITIONS]. The key lesson: [MAIN TAKEAWAY]."

**OUTRO (30 seconds)**
"What 30-day challenge should I try next? Comment below! Subscribe for more experiments. Full data in the description."
