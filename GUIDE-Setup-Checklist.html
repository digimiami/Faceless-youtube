SEO Optimization Checklist

Title Optimization

  • [ ] Under 60 characters
  • [ ] Main keyword at beginning
  • [ ] Power word included
  • [ ] Number or year (if relevant)
  • [ ] Creates curiosity

Description Optimization

  • [ ] Main keyword in first 25 words
  • [ ] 200+ words total
  • [ ] Timestamps added
  • [ ] Links to related videos
  • [ ] Social links included
  • [ ] Call-to-action at end

Tag Strategy

  • [ ] 1 main keyword (exact match)
  • [ ] 2-3 variations
  • [ ] 2-3 broader terms
  • [ ] 1-2 specific long-tail
  • [ ] No more than 8 tags

Thumbnail SEO

  • [ ] High CTR design
  • [ ] Consistent branding
  • [ ] Readable text
  • [ ] Emotion shown
  • [ ] Mobile-friendly
