## ğŸ¨ Design Specifications

### Text Guidelines
| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Font (primary) | Impact, Bebas Neue, Oswald Bold |
| Font (secondary) | Montserrat, Roboto |
| Max words | 5 words |
| Min size | 72pt |
| Outline | 4-6px stroke |
| Shadow | Drop shadow for depth |

### Color Combinations
| Mood | Background | Text | Accent |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| Urgent | #FF0000 | #FFFFFF | #FFFF00 |
| Trust | #0066CC | #FFFFFF | #00CCFF |
| Money | #00AA44 | #FFFFFF | #FFD700 |
| Mystery | #1A1A1A | #FFFFFF | #FF6600 |
| Happy | #FFCC00 | #000000 | #FF6600 |

### Image Guidelines
- **Resolution:** 1280x720 minimum (1920x1080 preferred)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File size:** Under 2MB
- **Main subject:** Occupy 60-70% of frame
- **Background:** Blur or solid (not distracting)
- **Faces:** Show emotion clearly

## ğŸ› ï¸ Canva Template Instructions

### How to Use
1. Open Canva (free version works)
2. Create custom size: 1280 x 720 pixels
3. Use templates above as reference
4. Customize with your images/colors
5. Download as PNG

### Free Resources
- **Unsplash:** Free photos
- **Pexels:** Free stock images
- **Canva Elements:** Built-in graphics
- **Remove.bg:** Remove backgrounds

### Pro Tips
1. Test thumbnails at small size (mobile)
2. Ask "Would I click this?"
3. A/B test different versions
4. Look at competitors for inspiration
5. One clear focal point only
