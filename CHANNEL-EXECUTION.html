Friday: Upload & Optimize (30 min) - Upload to YouTube - Add end screens + cards - Schedule or publish - Post to socials

THUMBNAIL FORMULA

Template Structure

  1. Background: Gradient or AI-generated scene
  2. Main image: Tool logo/screenshot or reaction face
  3. Big text: 3-5 words max, bold font

  5. Brand consistency: Same colors/fonts

  • Orange/Yellow: Attention, urgency
